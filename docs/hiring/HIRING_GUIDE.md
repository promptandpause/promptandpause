1. **Screening Call** (30 min) - Initial conversation
2. **Technical Interview** (60 min) - Skills assessment
3. **Cultural Fit Interview** (45 min) - Values and teamwork

- **Experience** (25%) - Relevant background
- **Cultural Fit** (20%) - Values alignment
- **Growth Potential** (15%) - Learning and adaptability

### **Offer Process**
1. **Reference Checks** - Professional references
2. **Background Check** - Where applicable
3. **Offer Discussion** - Compensation and expectations
4. **Onboarding Plan** - Integration and training
